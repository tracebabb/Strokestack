How it works

Up and running in 2 minutes

No setup headaches. No spreadsheets. Just golf.

01
Create your group
Add your regular playing partners and set their handicaps once. They're saved for every future round.
02
Pick your format
Choose Skins, Nassau, Wolf, Stroke Play, Stableford, or Closest to Pin — or stack multiple formats in one round.
03
Score hole by hole
Enter scores on your phone as you play. Everyone in the group sees live standings update in real time.
04
Get paid instantly
Round ends, StrokeStack calculates the net debts and fires off Venmo or PayPal requests automatically.
Features

Everything your foursome needs

Built by golfers who were tired of doing the math on the 19th hole.

Smart handicap engine
Enter handicaps once. StrokeStack auto-calculates fair strokes per player on every hole of every course — no arguments, no guesswork.
Live mobile scorecard
Enter scores hole by hole from anywhere on the course. The leaderboard updates live for everyone in the group simultaneously.
Auto Venmo & PayPal settlement
The moment your round ends, StrokeStack calculates every net debt across all formats and sends Venmo or PayPal requests. Money moves in minutes, not weeks.
Season leaderboard
Track wins, losses, and total money won or lost across every round all season long. Know once and for all who the real shark in your group is.
Game formats

Six classics. All automated.

Mix and match formats within a single round for maximum action.

FORMAT 01
Skins
Lowest net score wins the skin on each hole. Ties carry over, building the pot. The most exciting format in golf.
Most popular
FORMAT 02
Nassau
Three separate bets — front 9, back 9, and the full 18. Press options available for the gamblers in the group.
Classic
FORMAT 03
Wolf
Rotating team format where the Wolf chooses partners on each tee. The most social and strategic game in golf.
Team play
FORMAT 04
Stroke play
Total net score over 18 holes wins. The purest format — best player wins, no luck involved.
Pure golf
FORMAT 05
Stableford
Points-based system that rewards birdies and eagles while limiting the damage from blow-up holes.
High scores win
FORMAT 06
Closest to pin
Side pot on par 3s only. Everyone throws $5 in, whoever sticks it closest takes the whole pot.
Side action
